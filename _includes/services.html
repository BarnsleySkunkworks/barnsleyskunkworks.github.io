Who is it for?

If you have an interest in using technology solutions to improve the lives of council staff, town residents, businesses and visitors, then we'd love for you to get involved. Note though that for the moment the skunkworks is limited to employees of Barnsley MBC only. Once the honeymoon period passes and all the kinks are tweaked out of how the whole thing works, we do plan to open it up to the wider community. That said, we're on the lookout for...

Designers

Solutions need to be intuitive and designed to meet user needs

Developers

Join a team and bring prototypes to life

Engineers

Physical and connected devices will play a huge part in future service delivery

Ideas

Just have an idea? Bring it to the next session and tell us about it
